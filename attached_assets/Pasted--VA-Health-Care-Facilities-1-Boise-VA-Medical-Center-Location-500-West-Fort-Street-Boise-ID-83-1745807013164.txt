🧠 Mental Health & Crisis Support
Mental Health Services: Available at the Boise VA Medical Center and CBOCs, offering counseling, PTSD treatment, substance abuse programs, and more. ​

Veterans Crisis Line: 24/7 confidential support for veterans in crisis. Dial 988, then press 1.​

🎓 Education & Financial Assistance
State Education Benefits: Idaho offers educational assistance programs for veterans, including tuition waivers and scholarships. ​

Financial Assistance: Emergency grants and financial aid are available for veterans facing hardship. ​

⚖️ Legal Assistance
Idaho Legal Aid Services: Provides legal assistance to veterans on issues such as benefits, housing, and family law. ​
